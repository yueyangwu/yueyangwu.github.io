UTogether

A smartphone application, facilitates short-term meetups for UT students

Many international students at UT want to make friends with other local students so they joined events host by International Student and Scholar Services(ISSS) or other organizations on campus. ISSS offers friendship program and PALS program which matches international students with Austin community for an intercultural exchange of customs and lifestyles. However, arranging strangers in meetings is challenging. A more streamlined app, one that specifically addresses the challenges of on-the-fly social events, could ease this audienceâ€™s troubles.
