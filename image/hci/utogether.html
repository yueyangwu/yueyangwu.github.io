UTogether

A smartphone application, facilitates short-term meetups for UT students

Many international students at UT want to make friends with other UT students so they joined events host by International Student and Scholar Services(ISSS) or other organizations on campus. ISSS offers friendship program and PALS program which matches international students with Austin community for an intercultural exchange of customs and lifestyles. However, arranging strangers in meetings is challenging. A more streamlined app, one that specifically addresses the challenges of on-the-fly social events, could ease this audienceâ€™s troubles.

Storyboard

I drew the storyboard to help the team get a better understanding of how the user use the app. It tells story about how UTogether could help with arranging meeting planning and participant gathering in seconds. An initial user(the host) proposes an activity (coffee, jogging, chat, etc.) and timeframe. Users(the participants) search posted events with this metadata, or the app can pair compatible users automatically.
