PickGo

[Developing]A food pick up app for the customers and restaurants nearby.

Customers can order food through the app and pick up by themselves. The app could help customers to save time from wait in the lines, and provide them clearer and more convenient ordering experience. Also, the app profits the restaurants by helping them receive notice earlier and arrange the time to prepare for the order. Also, the app will be a good way to advertise the restaurants to potential customers.
